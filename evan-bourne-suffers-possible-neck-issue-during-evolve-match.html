Evan Bourne Suffers Possible Neck Issue During EVOLVE Match

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  173 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Matt Sydal performed at last night’s EVOLVE event as Evan Bourne for the first time in a long time. He was able to perform under that old name because of EVOLVE’s relationship with WWE.

PW Insider reports that Sydal was experiencing numbness in his arm during his match against Leon Ruff. This is not a good sign and they feared a neck injury.

Sydal reportedly walked out of the ring and was treated by medical staff who were at the event. He was said to be okay after the checkup.

He is still feeling some numbness in his arm so he might not wrestle at today’s EVOLVE event either.
