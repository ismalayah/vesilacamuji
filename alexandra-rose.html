Alexandra Rose From Netflix’s Selling the OC Is the Realtor Who Has Experience in Both Sales and Client Services!

Alexandra Rose, the cast member of Netflix’s Selling the OC, is the realtor whose skills truly cover every facet, allowing her to almost always successfully work. Given that the former has experience in both sales and client services, she is a dream team when it comes to bargaining for clients.

Rose was born on July 8, 1995, and is 27 years old as of 2022. In the trailer, Alexandra Rose is shown advising Alexandra Jarvis (@thealexandrajarvis) at one point. According to her LinkedIn profile, with her initial position with Sotheby’s International Realty in 2019, Rose appears to be one of the more recent real estate agents in terms of longevity.

Alexandra Rose hails from Orange County, according to her Oppenheim Group bio, though she moved to Dallas, Texas, with her family when she was 10 years old. Once she reached 18, Alexandra returned to Southern California and has lived there ever since.

With a background in sales and client services, Alexandra Rose enjoys providing customers with a specialized, expert home-buying experience. According to her biography on The Oppenheim Group website, Alexandra appreciates her retail and customer service experience; when she’s not showing customers luxurious properties, she likes to go hiking.

According to Alexandra’s LinkedIn profile, she earned a Bachelor’s in Finance and Economics from Grand Canyon University in 2019. Before the launch of Selling The OC, with nearly $100 million in sales in her first four years as a realtor and a degree in finance and economics, Alexandra brings an in-depth knowledge of the dynamic real estate market, enabling her to help her clients make decisions that are in line with their real estate objectives.

Her skills, understanding, and patience will make purchasing and selling a house a pleasant experience thanks to her professional history in sales and customer care, which guarantees her clients receive individualized counseling.

Every transaction benefits from Alexandra Rose’s local market knowledge, strong negotiating skills, and exceptional client service. She has built a solid reputation for her honesty, dependability, and professionalism. She takes great satisfaction in her ability to precisely complete a variety of duties while maintaining a happy outlook and putting her clients first.

Because they are not an all-female team, these agents will also be quite different from those we are accustomed to seeing on Selling Sunset. But since it’s Jason’s office, we should expect to see a lot of Jason on the show as well.
