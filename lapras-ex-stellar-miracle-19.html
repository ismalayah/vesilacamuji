Lapras (Japanese: ラプラスex Laplace ex) is a Water-type Basic Pokémon ex card. It is part of the Stellar Crown expansion.

Trivia

Origin

This card features a Terastallized Lapras with the Stellar Tera Type. The Larimar Rain attack is named after larimar, a rare gemstone variety of the mineral pectolite known for its bluish color.
