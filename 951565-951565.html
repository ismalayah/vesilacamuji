John Cena Heel Turn?

The brand new episode of WWE’s adult themed animated series, Camp WWE, debuted today on the WWE Network. In the new episode, John Cena, turns heel and performs the following heinous (and hilarious) activities (spoilers follow):

  • John Cena knocks Nikki Bella’s lunch tray out of her hands
  • John Cena snorts The Undertaker’s grandparents’ ashes
  • John Cena experiences a Fight Club/Tyler Durden-esque psychotic meltdown
  • John Cena hallucinates a demonic version of himself who falls to Earth in order to offer Cena his services
